Pete Delkus- Professional Career

Pete began his career as the head meteorologist at WCPO-TV, an ABC station, where he stayed for over a decade. During his stay there, he won the Associated Press Award for best regularly scheduled weatherman. After completing that, he joined WFAA-TV in mid-2005, and his spectacular journey to prominence began. Delkus is not only amusing and charming, as his followers describe him, but he is also knowledgeable in his subject, having received seals of approval from the National Weather Association and the American Meteorological Society.

His efforts have been acknowledged with Emmy nominations for “Outstanding Weather Anchor,” “Outstanding Host of a News Special,” “Outstanding Host of Special Event Coverage – Big D NYE,” and “Outstanding Weathercaster.” In addition, he was honored by the Associated Press and was named “Best Weathercaster” in Texas. Overall, as a hardworking and talented man, Pete has been able to gain recognition and rise to fame, and we are confident that his career will continue to flourish.
