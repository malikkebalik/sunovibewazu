Marlon Wayans' Only Son Shawn with Girlfriend Channel A$AP Rocky &amp; Zo Kravitz on Halloween

Actor and comedian Marlon Wayans recently shared a sweet post on his Instagram to show off the clever outfits that both his son, Shawn, and his girlfriend decided to wear for Halloween.

Marlon Wayans, actor, comedian, and proud father, recently took to his Instagram to dote on his son. The loving dad shared a photo of his son, Shawn, and his son's girlfriend, all dressed up for Halloween. He wrote:

"My son and his girl was @asaprocky and @zoeisabellakravitz i thought he was Keanu in the matrix."

Marlon Wayans on June 19, 2018 in Universal City, California | Photo: Getty Images

Wayans' fans and followers know how much he loves to dote on his children. Besides his son, Shawn, the 48-year-old is the proud father to his daughter, Amai.

Amai has had extra love and support from her famous Dad as she is openly gay. In June 2019, Wayans took to his Instagram as he always does to celebrate his daughter during pride month. He wrote:

"Happy pride ? to my pride and joy. I wouldn't change one thing about you. Love you to the moon around the sun through the galaxies and back again."

Wayans' social media is filled with many such posts in which he showed a tremendous amount of affection to his children. In a 2016 interview, the actor surprised and impressed his many fans when he shared his simple yet reflective way of being the best parent.

His advice was to change the things that did not work for him and to perfect the art of raising and loving the kids through his own experience.

Wayans' candid advice came just months before he released a family-friendly sitcom named "Marlon," that was loosely based on his breakup with his former wife, Angelica Zachary.

Wayans might be a single dad, but he sure is doing amazing at being the best father he can be.
