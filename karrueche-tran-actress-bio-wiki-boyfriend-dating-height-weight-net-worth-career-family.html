Karrueche Tran Career

  • As per her career, Tran began her career in the fashion industry as a personal shopper.
  • From 2009 to 2012, she worked at Nordstrom located at the Westfield Topanga mall in Canoga Park, CA.
  • Later, Tran worked as a freelance celebrity stylist in Hollywood.
  • In 2010, she first gained public attention when she began dating R&B artist Chris Brown.
  • Additionally, she began modeling by participating in photoshoots for local brands.
  • She was posed for brands like, Lady Crooks, the women’s division under the streetwear brand Crooks and Castles for their Summer 2013 collection.
  • She also began working on her own clothing line named, ‘The Kill’.
  • Tran gained wider prominence after starring as Vivian Johnson on the Emmy award-winning web series ‘The Bay from’ from 2013 to 2016.
  • She won two Daytime Emmy Awards, for producing the series.
  • She appeared in volume one of the photography book, Love West Coast Girls, by celebrity photographer Mike Miller, in 2014.
  • In the same year, she hosted the BET Awards red carpet and multiple events during the official BET Weekend.
  • Formerly, she had co-hosted the BET show 106 & Park.
  • In 2015, Tran was featured in the Spring/Summer lookbook for French-based athletic brand ‘Le Coq Sportif’.
  • She also appeared on the covers of several reputed magazines, including Rolling Out, Flaunt Magazine, Ouch Magazine, The Hundreds, Cliche Magazine, Bleu Magazine and Annex Magazine, including two feature stories on ELLE.com as well.
  • In 2015, her work led her to a contract with Wilhelmina Models LA, a big break through for her career.
  • Initially, she featured three films, 3-Headed Shark Attack followed by the lead in the short film PrXde.
  • Later, she gained recognition for her performance as ‘Vanessa Ivy’ in the fashion web series by ‘StyleHaul titled Vanity’, sponsored by the makeup brand Maybelline.
  • After supporting roles in ‘A Weekend with the Family’ and ‘The Nice Guys’, she landed the lead in ‘Only for One Night’.
  • In 2016, All three movies were released.
  • In the same year, she partnered up with ColourPop Cosmetics to release the limited edition cosmetic collaboration, KaePop.
  • In 2017, she began co-starring in ‘TNT Claws’.
  • She had joined the cast FOX’s Deputy in a recurring role playing ‘Genevieve’, in Oct 2019.
