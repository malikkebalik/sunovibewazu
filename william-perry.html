Biography

William Perry was born in Aiken, SC on December 16, 1962. Former Chicago Bears defensive tackle from 1985 to 1993. He helped the Bears win Super Bowl XX and retired with 29.5 sacks in 138 games. He scored a rushing touchdown in the Super Bowl when the team could have also given the ball to the legendary Walter Payton. He was a consensus first team All-American as a junior at Clemson.
