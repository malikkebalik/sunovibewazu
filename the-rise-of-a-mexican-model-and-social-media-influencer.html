The Rise of A Mexican Model And Social Media Influencer

Maya Nazor is a Mexican model and social media influencer who gained fame through Instagram, boasting over 6 million followers where she shares modeling and lifestyle content.

Beyond social media, Nazor’s work extends to print media. She made her debut in Revista magazine, showcasing her presence in the world of fashion. Additionally, the Mexican celebrity has appeared in the music video “Santa Fe Klan: Luka,” further expanding her reach beyond the realms of social media.

Early Life & Family

Maya Nazor was born on January 5, 1999, in Cuernavaca, Mexico. That means she’s in her mid-twenties now.

Maya has been quite private about her early life and hasn’t shared much about her family. However, in a TikTok video, some female family members, including her mother Cristina Nazor, made an appearance.

Together, Cristina and Maya did the “mother and daughter tag.” Despite Maya’s preference for keeping her personal life under wraps, her love for her family shines through.

Know About Maya Nazor Sisters

Similar to Maya Nazor, her sisters Farah and Ximena Nazor are also involved in modeling. Their modeling and lifestyle content has gained them a considerable following.

Ximena and Farah both boast over 200,000 followers on Instagram. While Maya and Farah seem to be single, their sister Ximena is in a relationship with a content creator named Axel Ortiz Moran.

Apart from Farah and Ximena, Maya has half-siblings. Her father has two children from his previous relationships.

Is Maya Nazor on Relationship?

Currently, Maya Nazor is not dating anyone. The Mexican model is focused on raising her child as a single mother. However, she was previously in a high-profile relationship with the Mexican singer and rapper Santa Fe Klan.

Maya’s ex-boyfriend, Santa Fe Klan aka Ángel Jair Quezada Jasso has moved on. Since, He is romantically involved with Karely Ruiz. During a concert, Santa Fe Klan invited Karely Ruiz on stage and shared a kiss.

Maya Nazor And Santa Fe Klan Relationship Journey

In August 2021, Maya and Santa Fe Klan became a couple. Despite following each other on social media, they hadn’t spoken until then. When Maya Nazor finally met the Mexican rapper, she was impressed by his kind heart, finding many common interests. Over time, they developed mutual feelings and started dating.

As for the reason behind their breakup, Maya hasn’t provided details, and neither has Santa Fe Klan. A Single Mother

After parting ways with her former partner, Maya Nazor is now navigating the journey of motherhood as a single mom. She welcomed her child, Luka, into the world on August 23, 2022.

Luka was just four months old when Maya and Santa Fe Klan separated. Despite the changes, Maya takes pride in being a mom. She shared a heartfelt picture of herself kissing her little one, captioned: “For all my life You are part of me, I love you my little baby.”

Despite the separation, Santa Fe Klan and Maya still maintain communication for the sake of their child. Rumors circulated that Nazor was seeking financial support from the Mexican rapper, but as of now, there is no confirmation or sufficient evidence to support this claim.
