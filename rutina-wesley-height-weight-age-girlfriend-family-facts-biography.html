Education

Rutina Wesley graduated from Las Vegas Academy of International Studies, Performing and Visual Arts in 1997 and she also learned dancing at Simba Studios. Later, she joined the University of Evansville in Indiana and obtained her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre Performance in 2001.

She went on to pursue her artistic dream and joined Juilliard School in New York in 2001, graduating in May 2005. During her time at Julliard School, she also did a summer course at the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art.
