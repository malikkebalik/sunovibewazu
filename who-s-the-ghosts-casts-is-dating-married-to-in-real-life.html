Who's the Ghosts Casts Is Dating, Married To in Real Life?

Credit: Courtesy of Getty/Instagram

  • Who is the Ghosts cast dating and married to?

    Ghosts deserve a chance at love too, right? From Flower and Thor to Hetty and Trevor, Ghosts has introduced several romances over its three-season run. But in the stars’ personal lives, who is the Ghosts cast dating and married to in real life? Read on to learn more about their off-screen relationships and families at home. 

    Ghosts, an American sitcom adapted from the British series of the same name, first premiered on CBS in 2021. Rose McIver and Utkarsh Ambudkar star as Samantha and Jay Arondekar, married New Yorkers who inherit an upstate mansion and want to turn it into a bed and breakfast. After Samantha suffers a near-death accident, she can see the ghosts who call the mansion home. The spirits inhabiting the house, which include a Native American, a Viking, and a Prohibition jazz singer, died on the property and are bound to the area until they reach the afterlife. Ghosts is currently in its third season.

    Who is the Ghosts cast dating? Read on to learn about the real-life partners of your favorite characters in the CBS comedy, including Rose Mclver, Utkarsh Ambudkar, Danielle Pinnock, Sheila Carrasco, and more.

  • Rose McIver (Samantha “Sam” Arondekar)

    Lead star Rose Mclver is married to Australian photographer and visual artist George Byrne. According to Bryne’s website, he was born in Sydney in 1976 and graduated from Sydney College Of The Arts in 2001. Bryne has been living in Los Angeles since 2011.

    After six years of dating, the lovebirds tied the knot on January 2, 2023. One year later, the couple revealed that they were expecting! Rose debuted her baby bump at the 2024 Golden Globes. She shared a photo to her Instagram page before heading to the star-studded event. “Mum and dad went out,” she penned in the caption. “Huge congrats to Anatomy of a Fall, it was an honor to present Justine Triet the much deserved win! ❤️ @goldenglobes @cbstv.”

  • Utkarsh Ambudkar (Jay Arondekar)

    Ghosts star Utkarsh Ambudkar is married to his wife, Naomi. The pair tied the knot on September 9, 2019. Ambudkar posted two photos of the special day, including one picture wearing traditional garments and another with modern ones. He captioned the happy photos simply with a ring emoji.

    The actor posted a tribute to his wife for their fourth wedding anniversary with a slideshow of photos. To quote my wife: “Till death do us part, muthafucka” Happy Anniversary to the baddest queen in the solar system 🤎” he penned in the caption. Utkarsh and Naomi have three children together: Tiare (age 8), Bhumi (age 3), and Lesina (9 months). They welcomed their eldest daughter, Tiara, before they were married.

  • Danielle Pinnock (Alberta Haynes)

    Ghosts actress Danielle Pinnock is married to Jack Wallace. The couple’s first wedding took place in 2013 in a New Jersey intensive care unit after Pinnock’s mother, Joan, suffered a stroke the night before the wedding.

    “I’m so grateful that I’m married to the man that I’m married to, because even in just awful situations and stressful situations, he’s always been calm,” she told People. “He’s just always been steady. Jack was like, ‘You know what? We’re going to do the wedding here.’ And I was like, ‘In the ICU?’ He was like, ‘It’s fine. We’re just going to pivot.’ So I was like, ‘Okay.'”

    The couple celebrated a decade of marriage on April 8, 2023, with a special vow renewal ceremony. “I don’t think one person ate at the wedding,” she recalled to the site. “We were just dancing and drinking … Just celebrating that my mom was alive, our nuptials. And that, honestly, for me, that original wedding just showed me that these were the biggest hardships of the beginning of our marriage.”

  • Richie Moriarty (Peter “Pete” Martino)

    Richie Moriarty is married to Ciara Moriarty, a former actress who has appeared in Law & Order: SVU, Angel, and Cold Case. On Richie’s website, he shared that he co-starred with his wife on a show called Maybe Baby, which they co-wrote and performed at UCBT Hell’s Kitchen.

    Richie and Ciara have two children together, but the Ghosts star has kept his family out of the public eye, especially his children. He did share a sweet photo of him and his wife in February 2021. “I’m the luckiest 🦆. Love you @ciaraville!” he wrote.

  • Asher Grodman (Trevor Lefkowitz)

    Asher Grodman has kept his love life out of the public eye, but he was last linked to Broadway actress Samanta Massell. According to Samatha’s website, she is “an actress, singer, and writer based in New York City. She has appeared on Broadway, on television, in films, in regional theater, alongside symphonies, and in a variety of commercials.”

    Last year, Grodman posted a photo of himself and Samatha while traveling Europe. “Europe with my #1—love you!” he wrote on August 5, 2023.

  • Sheila Carrasco (Susan “Flower” Montero)

    Sheila Carrasco is married to her husband, Josh. The couple tied the knot on October 9, 2017. In July 2023, Sheila announced that she and Josh were expecting their first child together. The couple welcomed their baby girl, Mirentxu Palmer Carrasco Stamell, on November 28, 2023. 

    “Our beautiful bb is 3 months old!! She is lit from within and smiling, chatting up a storm, sitting upright, teething, and loving her books!” she wrote on Instagram in March 2024. “Her laughter is the greatest sound in the universe and her tears make me wanna move mountains. Josh is Dadding above and beyond, and the two of them are thick as thieves. Best and hardest 3 months of my life so far, and it’s only just begun. I can’t wait for more.”

  • Devan Chandler Long (Thorfinn “Thor”)

    Devan Chandler Long is married to his wife, Jesse Golden. Golden is the CEO of The Golden Secrets, a holistic and sustainable skincare and wellness line. The couple reportedly tied the knot in a courthouse ceremony in August 2020.

    “Happy Name Day to my beautiful brilliant best friend momma wife, life adventure companion and season pass holder to endless home cooked meals, cozy fireplaces and terrible dad dances. Love you hun ♥️,” Long wrote about his wife on Instagram in December 2023.

    In November 2023, the paired announced that they were expecting their firstborn. “Yes! we have a turkey in the oven☺️and we are so thrilled to finally share and celebrate something that we have been looking forward to forever. Thank you for being on this journey with us.🙏🏽” Golden wrote on Instagram alongside a video of her and Devan.

  • Rebecca Wisocky (Henrietta “Hetty” Woodstone)

    Ghosts actress Rebecca Wisocky married her husband, award-winning theatrical lighting designer Lap Chi Chu, in October 2015. “I am marrying my best friend and quite honestly the best man I’ve ever known,” Wisocky told People at the time. “I can’t believe my good fortune to find this happiness. It’s never too late.”

    The couple tied the knot at an older mansion in Boston, followed by a traditional Chinese tea ceremony and banquet at Boston’s iconic China Pearl restaurant. “So many people we love have come from all over the world to celebrate with us,” she also told the site. “We are humbled with hearts bursting to share this day with them.”
