Terry Crews Does A Thousand Miles At The Piano Hollywood Life

‘Lip Sync Battle’: Terry Crews Does ‘A Thousand Miles’

Duh na na na na na na, and I need you.

Don’t lie, you remember all the words. Apparently, so does Terry Crews! The actor took on the song as his selection on the most recent episode of Lip Sync Battle, and it was perfect.

Clad in a head to toe white suit, Terry sat at the piano and gave the audience some serious face. But it gets better: When he arrived at the chorus (“if I could fall”), Terry actually got up from the piano and removed his blazer! A couple ballerinas grabbed the coat, exposing Terry’s bare (and perfect) abs, and then he proceeded to perform ballet moves all around the stage! Oh, and there’s also ribbon dancing involved.

Terry Crews & Mike Tyson Face Off

While Terry’s performance was certainly one for the ages, he had some serious competition. Enter: Mike Tyson!

Mike took on the Salt N Peppa classic, “Push It,” and he totally nailed the 80s realness, wearing a red leather jacket, a massive gold chain and some very tight leather pants.

Well, it’s safe to say, when it comes to both Terry and Mike’s performances, we’re all winners here!

Who gave the best performance, Terry or Mike?
