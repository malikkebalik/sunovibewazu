Billboard Music Awards red carpet best-dressed: From Kylie Jenner to Megan Fox wow fans

The 2022 Billboard Music Awards have started, and the red carpet showcased some of the season's most eccentric outfits. The biggest stars in music gathered at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas for the 2022 Billboard Music Awards on Sunday, May 15, and they did so in style.

The annual awards event, hosted by Sean 'Diddy' Combs, did not disappoint on the red carpet, with Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly, Kylie Jenner, and Megan Thee Stallion making everyone drool with their show-stopping outfits. The A-list provided us with everything from traditional black to rainbow hues! Take a look at some of the best-dressed celebs at the Billboard Music Awards below.

Megan Fox & Machine Gun Kelly

Megan and her boyfriend MGK walked the red carpet and Megan looked amazing in a David Koma gown with a deep plunging neckline, a daring thigh split, built-in gloves, and rhinestone flowers at the shoulders. Meanwhile, MGK glammed up his Dolce & Gabbana suit with ornate spikes, a gleaming silver turtleneck embroidered with Swarovski crystals, and a $30K diamond manicure.

Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott

Kylie Jenner was there to show support for Travis Scott and looked stunning in a curve-hugging, tie-dye-style Balmain gown. The Kylie Cosmetics creator donned a blue and grey long-sleeve gown for the event, while Scott chose a green suit. Stormi, the couple's daughter, was also present. 

Doja Cat

Rapper Doja Cat then made a statement in a striking Schiaparelli FW22 Couture black dress that showed off her chest. She completed the look with a gold planet-inspired purse and her hair in a slicked-back ponytail.

Megan Thee Stallion

In an ombre crop top and matching tiny skirt, Megan Thee Stallion flaunted her belly. The rapper donned a tan and brown-hued crop top with a distinctive accent over her shoulders, along with a matching skirt, smoky eye makeup, and sleek hair, and finished off her look with eye-catching shoes. Megan's train was held aloft for her on the carpet by model Cara Delevingne, who then let it go and let it flutter back to the carpet for a sweet moment.

Dove Cameron

Singer and actress Dove Cameron looked stunning in an Ashlyn gown with a dramatic red pleated full skirt.

Heidi Klum

Heidi Klum wore a bright yellow Toni Maticevski strapless dress with an off-center slit, which she paired with black thigh-high boots and opera gloves. 

Lainey Wilson

For the Billboard Music Awards, the country singer wore metallic grey bellbottom slacks with a flowery black, green, and pink jacket. She accessorized with a belt and a large floral pin on her lapel.

Becky G

The artist wore a purple short dress with a one-shoulder feathery design to the awards presentation.

Elle King

Elle King wore a black figure-hugging dress that was traditional and stylish. 

Michael Bublé & Luisana Lopilato

Bublé wore a blue suit to the red carpet. He stood with Luisana Lopilato, who donned a blue dress with a feathered skirt, prior to the awards event.

The Billboard Charts have been the standard for rating the popularity of artists, songs, and albums since 1940, and they are the ultimate measure of musical success. The nominees and winners of the Billboard Music Awards are determined by major fan engagements with music, such as album and digital song sales, streaming, radio airplay, and touring.
