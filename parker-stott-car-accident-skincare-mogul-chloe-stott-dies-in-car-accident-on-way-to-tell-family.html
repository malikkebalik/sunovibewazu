Parker Stott Car Accident; Skincare mogul Chloe Stott dies in car accident on way to tell family

Skincare mogul Chloe Stott and her unborn son lost their lives in a tragic car accident while returning home to Arizona for Christmas. The accident occurred near Wickenburg, Arizona, leaving Chloe’s husband, Parker, in critical condition after being emergency airlifted to the Arizona Burn Center. The couple had been on their way from Utah to Arizona to celebrate the joyous news of expecting their first child with their families.

Chloe, the visionary founder and CEO of Clover Skin Bar in Lindon, Utah, established herself as a prominent figure in the skincare industry. The spa, located approximately 40 minutes south of Salt Lake City, flourished under Chloe’s leadership. In 2022, she and Parker expanded their business, creating Clover Skin Bar and assembling a team of skilled estheticians specializing in age prevention and acne treatment.

The heartbreaking news of Chloe’s untimely demise has sent shockwaves through the skincare community. In response to the tragedy, Clover Skin Bar announced a temporary closure on its Instagram page, expressing the need for time to process the unexpected loss. The post garnered an outpouring of condolences from followers and fans alike.

Social media became a platform for tributes and expressions of grief as news of Chloe’s passing spread. Dallas Aesthetician Savanna Boda shared a touching tribute on Chloe’s Instagram page, saying, “Rest in peace Chloe. You will be missed.” Fellow influencer Emilie Kiser paid her respects in an Instagram story, emphasizing the fragility of life and sending love to Chloe, now considered “a true Angel.” Skincare expert and influencer Jenna Rammell echoed the sentiment, expressing a broken heart and conveying immense love for Chloe.

Chloe’s final Instagram post, featuring a picture of her and Parker with the caption “You n Me forever Parker,” now takes on a poignant significance. Fans and well-wishers flooded the comments section, offering their condolences and remembering Chloe for the positive impact she had on the skincare community.

As the public mourns this tragic loss, there remains an air of uncertainty as official reports of Chloe’s death have yet to be confirmed. The US Sun has reached out to the Wickenburg Police Department for comment, seeking clarity on the details surrounding the accident.

In the midst of the grieving process, a GoFundMe campaign has been initiated to provide financial support to Parker and the young family left behind. The skincare community and Chloe’s followers are rallying together to offer assistance during this challenging time, highlighting the camaraderie and support that extends beyond the realm of business.

Chloe Stott leaves behind a legacy as a trailblazer in the skincare industry, her influence reaching far beyond the walls of Clover Skin Bar. The tragedy serves as a stark reminder of the fragility of life and the importance of cherishing every moment with loved ones. As the skincare world mourns the loss of one of its own, Chloe’s memory will undoubtedly endure through the lives she touched and the impact she made on the beauty community.
