Chrissy Teigen from 'Chrissy's Court' Shares Hot Swimsuit Snap with Her New Dog Petey

Oh, to be a dog by the poolside of Chrissy Teigen. The "Chrissy's Court" star recently shared a hot swimsuit shot with her new dog, Petey.

Teigen managed to enjoy her quarantined life with a new family member. As proof, she posted a "thirst trap" photo on Instagram together with Petey the poodle.

The image shows Teigen wearing a sexy black one-piece swimsuit and playing with Petey. "Me and Petey being thirst traps," the model and TV personality wrote in her post.

Chrissy Teigen and John Legend at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on February 09, 2020 in Beverly Hills, California. | Photo: Getty Images

ADOPTED PETEY

Teigen and husband John Legend adopted Petey back in January with the help of Ellen DeGeneres. The brown poodle was adopted from a rescue shelter, as reported by ET Online.

From being a puppy, Petey has grown into an adorable dog. Petey is beautiful and sweet, but not the brightest, according to Teigen.

"He's the only dog that doesn't like water, and we have a three-legged dog that can swim better than Petey," Teigen said. "He gets super scared so we don't want to force it on him."

COPING WITH QUARANTINE

Aside from Petey's comforting presence, Teigen and Legend are making other ways of coping with quarantine. They shared how they have dealt with the social distancing amid the Coronavirus outbreak, as reported by Closer Weekly.

Remaining calm, Teigen said the present situation is unusual for both of them as they are used to be busy with work. Right now, they are taking things slowly day by day and making the most out of it.

THROWBACK TO HAPPIER DAYS

Legend encouraged others to check on their family members during this difficult time. Just like his family, others are also trying to get through the situation.

Being quarantined has made people reminisce about the old days. Teigen, for one, posted a throwback photo of herself celebrating the 10th anniversary of her breast implants, with a funny caption, as reported by People.

Teigen candidly said that she went under the knife at 20, when she started runway modeling. Her hilarious post led Kim Kardashian to comment with emoji faces.

Comedian Whitney Cummings also commented about taking her breast implants out. Teigen replied that she can't remove it just yet because of the quarantine.

Before the global Coronavirus outbreak worsened, Legend and his family went to Thailand for a vacation. He posted several photos of Teigen and their two beautiful children enjoying their family holiday.
