Taylor Swift concert tickets sell for $15K at Selena Gomez auction

Explore More

Sold! To the Swiftie with $15,000 to spare.

A pair of tickets to any Taylor Swift concert in the world sold for $15,000 at Selena Gomez’s charity auction Wednesday night.

The Rare Impact Fund Benefit benefited the “Come & Get It” singer’s mission to help people struggling with mental health issues and self-acceptance.

Gomez’s “Only Murders in the Building” co-star Martin Short, 73, emceed the event and started the bidding for the Swift tickets at $5,000.

The jokester entertained the crowd while bidding for the tickets, quipping that $6,500 was just the price for valet parking at a Swift show. 

The winning bidder dished out three times that amount, months after Swift’s Eras Tour, which is currently on hiatus, broke Ticketmaster, upsetting fans — and the “Anti-Hero” singer herself.

The tour resumes on Nov. 17 for three shows in Brazil before pausing again until February.

Taylor’s tickets were the highest-selling item of the night.

Famous faces, including Tyga, H.E.R. and Francia Raisa, Gomez’s friend and kidney donor, attended the benefit.

The Post has contacted reps for Gomez, 31, and Swift, 33, for comment.

Swift and Gomez have been friends since they both dated two different Jonas Brothers at the same time in 2008.

The “Better Than Revenge” songstress is currently lending her Tribeca apartment to Sophie Turner as her nasty divorce from Swift’s ex, Joe Jonas, rages on.
