Who Is Ben Maddison? James Maddison Brother: Age Gap And Family Tree

Does James Maddison of soccer have a brother? Fans of the soccer genius are curious to know about his familial ties. 

James Daniel Maddison is an English professional soccer player who plays as an attacking midfielder for Premier League club Leicester City and the England national team. He was born on November 23, 1996, in Coventry, England.

Maddison began his career with Coventry City before joining Norwich City in 2016. He joined Premier League Club Leicester City in 2018.

He has made over 201 appearances for the club, winning the FA Cup and the FA Community Shield, also representing Leicester City in European competition.

Maddison wears jersey no. 10 for the club and 25 for his national team. He has been able to score 56 goals for Leicester City.

The Coventry-born midfielder is renowned as a free-kick specialist, often scoring extraordinary goals. He has a whopping net worth of about $20 million. 

James played for England in the under-21 category from 2017 to 2019 and made his debut for the senior team in 2019.

He also had active participation in the 2022 FIFA World Cup at Qatar. 

Meet James Maddison Brother, Ben Maddison

The Leicester City midfielder James has a brother named Ben Maddison, who is younger than him.

On May 8, 2022, James made an Instagram post wishing his younger sibling Ben a happy 22nd birthday.

Ben was born on May 8, 2000. That means he is three and a half years younger than his elder sibling James Maddison.

However, not many other details about Jame’s younger brother are available on the internet.

It seems that Ben is a huge supporter of James. He even idolizes his big brother.
