Difference Between Beretta 92Fs And Beretta M9

The Beretta 92F is a single-action, semi-automatic pistol with an open-top slide and a double-action trigger. The Beretta M9, on the other hand, is a double-action, semi-automatic pistol with a closed-top slide and a single-action trigger. Caliber

The Beretta 92F is available in 9mm Luger, .40 S&W and .45 ACP. The Beretta M9 is only available in the 9mm Luger caliber. However, the M9 can be converted to fire the .40 S&W and .45 ACP calibers with the use of an aftermarket conversion kit.
