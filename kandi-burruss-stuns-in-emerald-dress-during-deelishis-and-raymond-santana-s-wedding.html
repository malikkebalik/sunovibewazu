Kandi Burruss Stuns in Emerald Dress during Deelishis and Raymond Santana's Wedding

Kandi Burruss dazzled her social media timeline as she stepped out in a hot number and flaunted her curves in a green dress.

Kandi Burruss looked picture-perfect in a recent Instagram share where she looked stunning as a wedding guest.

Kandi Burruss visits SiriusXM Studios on March 5, 2018 in New York City | Photo: Getty Images

This past weekend, the media star was in attendance at model Deelishis and film producer Raymond Santana's wedding. The "Xscape" star wore her brunette hair in a lengthy style while she adorned it with some stylish hairpins.

[Kandi Burrus] opened up that after leaving Xscape, her career as a solo artist wasn't as impressive as she envisioned.

Her ample curves were clad in a figure-hugging green dress as she completed the look with perfect makeup and some sparkly jewelry. In one of her Instagram posts, Burruss stood between the bride and groom, as they all smiled and posed for the camera.

While Deelishis sported a white bouquet, Kandi held some beautiful red roses. The 44-year-old gleefully wrote in her caption:

"Congratulations @Santanaraymond & @iamsodeelishes, I'm so happy for y'all, and I'm happy to say I had a little something to do with this love connection."

The second post showed Kandi posing with the bride while telling "fellas" in the caption that the bride was "off the market!"

Hollywood Life shares that Deelishis, whose real name is London Charles, was a sight to behold on her big day as she sported a form-fitting immaculate white dress with a long train.

Also in attendance at Deelishis' weddings was fellow star Shamea Morton and Kandi's spouse, Todd Tucker.

Page Six reports that the RHOA celebrity might be opting for a new style trend for her hair! Burruss's daughter, Riley, recently did the high chop and had been rocking her natural curls in the most stylish ways.

This seems to be a point of inspiration for "mama" Kandi who's been hinting on trying out a new look for her hair. She told the news outlet:

"...I feel like it's always cool when a woman can embrace her natural hair and really feel good about it. I want to do the same thing."

Recently, the businesswoman bagged a new accolade as the winner of "The Masked Singer." Kandi emerged as the winner for the third season, and as reported by People, she revealed that her new achievement boosted her confidence.

The songstress opened up that after leaving Xscape, her career as a solo artist wasn't as impressive as she envisioned. However, she gained renewed confidence in herself following her success as "The Masked Singer."

Kandi couldn't keep the excitement from her voice as she told People how she felt about being the champion. The Grammy-winner was even more exhilarated when she learned that she was the first female to win!
