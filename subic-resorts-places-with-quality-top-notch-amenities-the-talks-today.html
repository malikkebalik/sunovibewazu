Subic Beach Resorts – Where To Stay For Your Next Staycation?

Here are some Subic beach resorts you can stay at on your next vacation.

SUBIC BEACH RESORTS – We all need a good time by the beach with some great food and here are some resorts that will give you these.

A no-fail destination for people looking for a food trip, shopping, beach escapades, and sightseeing is Subic. This is a famous tourist destination in the north for a quick getaway. And if you happen to desire to go there, let’s help in planning for your much-needed break.

Subic, Zambales is 84-kilometer drive from Manila, and here are the best places where you can stay in this thriving coastal town:

  • The Lighthouse Marina Resort
    The best part about this resort is that it is in a building with Subic’s famous lighthouse. This is located in Moonbay Marina Complex in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone and each room is both dreamy and homey which is perfect for all kinds of travelers no matter where they have come from.
  • ACEA Subic Bay
    This idyllic resort is among the vast and luxurious beach resorts in the Subic Bay Freeport Zone that is near several famous spots like Dungaree Beach, All Hands Beach, Subic Bay International Airport, and Ocean Adventure.
  • Mangrove Resort Hotel
    This is located along Olongapo’s Baloy Long Beach Road. This is a peaceful refuge with a view of the sea and it is conveniently distant from the noise of the city.
  • Kamana Sanctuary Resort and Spa
    As the name sounds, this is an oasis where peace and luxury meet. It is promising as this is more than just a resort. It is also a place where you can relax and connect with nature at the same time. This is located in Nabasan Beach and near spots like Ocean Adventure, Zoobic Safari, and Tree Top Adventure Subic.
  • Camayan Beach Resort Hotel
    This place has exciting sights and attractions to offer. It is also practically near Ocean Adventure and Zoobic Safari. Five kilometers from here is the Tree Top Adventure Subic where you can enjoy activities like canopy rides, trekking, and interactive freefalls.
