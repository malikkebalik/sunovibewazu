Jessica Szhor Pics Photos Of The Actress Hollywood Life

Jessica Szohr arrives at the Kaos Dayclub and Nightclub Grand Opening Weekend At Palms Casino Resort on April 5, 2019 in Las Vegas, Nevada. She wore a white top, black hat, and a pair of stylish earrings.

Jessica is best known for her role as Vanessa Abrams on the CW’s ‘Gossip Girl.’ She starred alongside Blake Lively, Leighton Meester, Chace Crawford, Penn Badgley, Ed Westwick, and Taylor Momsen. Jessica has also appeared in the shows ‘Twin Peaks,’ ‘Shameless,’ and ‘The Orville.’ In film, she’s starred in ‘I Don’t Know How She Does It,’ ‘The Internship,’ and ‘Ted 2.’ Jessica started dating NHL star Brad Richardson in 2019. The couple welcomed their first child together, daughter Bowie Ella, in January 2021. Jessica and Brad officially got engaged in May 2022.
