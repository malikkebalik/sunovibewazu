Ashley Tisdale Shares First Photos of Her Adorable Baby Girl Jupiter

Actress Ashley Tisdale shared pictures of her daughter’s face for the first time on the occasion of Mother’s Day. She posted several pictures of her daughter, Jupiter Iris French, in several cute outfits alongside a snap of her husband together with Jupiter.

Tisdale also showed appreciation to all mothers in the caption, calling them “goddesses” and referring to single moms as “superheroes.” Jupiter was born on March 23, and the High School Musical alum said the six weeks since her daughter’s arrival had been a blessing.

Tisdale had previously shared pictures of her daughter on Instagram but hadn't shown the newborn’s face. A bunch of the actress’s friends gushed over Jupiter’s photo on the comment section, including Blake Lively and Amanda Kloots.

High School Musical director Kenny Ortega also dropped by, wishing Tisdale a happy mother’s day. 

The actress also celebrated moms everywhere when talking with Forbes recently, where she talked about her sister — a single mother for over a decade now.

They are my superheroes. Becoming a mom, you really don’t realize how hard it is. It’s amazing, it’s awesome, but it is definitely not the easiest thing in the world.

Married To Christopher French Since 2014

Tisdale and Christopher French, a music composer, met each other back in 2012. Eight months later, the duo got engaged on top of the Empire State Building. The couple tied the knot on September 8, 2014, in Santa Barbara, California.

Just a few days after celebrating their sixth marriage anniversary, Tisdale announced her pregnancy by showing off her baby bump together with French on September 17, 2020.

The couple was dressed in all-white, posing with lush greenery behind them.

Love At First Sight

In 2013, Tisdale revealed in an interview with On Air With Ryan Seacrest that she knew he was the one for her immediately.

I’ve obviously been in long relationships in the past. But I think because of knowing what I wanted, I wouldn’t have gone into a relationship if it wasn’t what I wanted. I think from the moment that him and I met, I just knew it.

French also produced Tisdale’s 2013 song, ‘You’re Always Here.' Tisdale said his involvement in the production process was one reason why she was sure of him as her long-term partner. French has collaborated with Tisdale musically on other projects besides the song, often appearing on her YouTube channel for her music sessions series. 

The married couple has done covers of some of their favorite songs like Paramore’s ‘Still Into You’ and Walk The Moon’s ‘Shut Up and Dance.’
