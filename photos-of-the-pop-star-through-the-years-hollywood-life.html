Photos Of The Pop Star Through The Years Hollywood Life

There really is no one like Madonna. Whether it’s her fashion sense or her music, the talented Pop Queen has a style and sound all her own. Over the years, she’s continued to innovate, going from a dance music star in the ’80s to one of the most recognized vocalists of all time.

Madonna first broke onto the scene in the early ‘80s with hit songs like ‘Holiday,’ ‘Borderline’, and more. It wasn’t before long that her 1984 album ‘Like A Virgin’ catapulted her into pop music icon status. The star never shied away from controversy, causing outrage with her music videos for ‘Like A Prayer’ and later on in the ’90s with ‘Justify My Love’.

The ‘Material Girl’ is still making music and moves in the entertainment industry, with a huge tour set to kick off in 2023. Unfortunately, though, her tour was delayed on June 28, 2023 when her manager, Guy Oseary, announced that she had suffered a medical complication. A full recovery was made and she kicked off her ‘Celebration’ tour in London in October 2023. Check out the gallery to see photos of Madonna over the years!
