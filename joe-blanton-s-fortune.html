Joseph Matthew Blanton: A Wiki Biography

Joseph Matthew Blanton, born on December 11, 1980, is a former American professional baseball pitcher. Following his time at the University of Kentucky, Blanton was chosen by the Oakland Athletics and competed for numerous Major League Baseball (MLB) teams from 2004 to 2013. Notably, during the 2008 World Series while playing for the Philadelphia Phillies, Blanton not only secured a home run but also emerged as the victorious pitcher in the fourth game.
