From Secret Engagement to Dating History

Everything to know about Ellie Goulding's married life: The singer-songwriter is a mother to one!  

Ellie Goulding's arrival and sweep in the music industry was unannounced. The Grammy-nominated crooner began her ascend with her debut song, Under the Sheets, in 2009, and through an apparent figure compared to her fellow pop stars, Goulding has been only climbing her way up since.  

Born on December 30, 1986, in Hereford, England, Ellie's electropop, and indie-pop songs have kept us entertained for more than a decade. The Brit, a soprano at root, has released a myriad of chart-topping hits while keeping it relatively away from the glitz and glare of the glamour world. Here's everything to know about her married life.  

Inside Ellie Goulding's Marital Life!   

As she navigated a soaring career in the industry, Ellie Goulding is also anchoring a thriving personal life. The once-eligible bachelorette is now a happy wife and doting mother. 

Goulding married art dealer Caspar Jopling in 2019 and has been living a quiet, off-the-grid life with him. The Brit and her leading man were first linked in April 2017. 

After little to no substantial evidence of their romance, the buzz shifted and so did the curiosity. But then came the sudden engagement announcement from the Love Me Like You Do hitmaker in August 2018.  

The low-profile couple's engagement was announced via The Times. While disclosure posed many questions for the newly engaged, they held their ground and carried on with their away-from-the-spotlight romance. 

Ellie Goulding's Wedding Was A Spectacle!

The singer and art dealer pair tied the knot on August 31, 2019, at York Minster. The duo's extravagant nuptial was a star-studded affair. Ellie Goulding and her beau, Casper Jopling, wedded in a wedding of the decades with varying stars as an attendee!

Yet the guest list was one packed with star power! The attendees for the wedding include Princess Beatrice, Edoardo Mapelli Mozzi, Princess Eugenie, Jack Brooksbank, Katy Perry, Orlando Bloom, and more.

Although the pair hardly talk about their life as a married couple, both Ellie and Caspar have made some exceptions over the years.

More recently, the English art dealer gushed about his famous wife in an Instagram post. Jopling wrote a heartfelt appreciation for Goulding and expressed his ever-growing love for her. 

"I love you even more than I did a year ago; thanks a million for everything you do. You're pretty f**** grand," the caption read. 

The Close to Me singer has also reflected on her love story a handful of times. In 2020, Goulding unpacked the beginning of her what appeared to be a whirlwind romance. 

The singer said it took her and Jopling months before getting comfortable around each other. Likewise, Ellie and Casper started dating after many meets and greets.  

Learn about Ellie Goulding's Husband and Their Kids!

Jopling, needless to say, comes from an affluent background. He may be known by his wife's credits worldwide, but it could be the other way around in the duo's native.

Inside Ellie Goulding's Dating History!  

Fans would recall when Goulding's love life sparked as much buzz as any other star. The Grammy-nominated crooner might have come far and beyond those days, but the stories are still one click away. The Brit's last boyfriend happens to be her former trainer, Bobby Rich.   

Goulding and Rich began a hasty romance in late 2016, and by early 2017, it fizzled out. Ellie's fair share of romances also includes unconfirmed rumored affairs. She was famously linked with Prince Harry in 2016.  

Goulding's other notable relationship was with former McFly band member Dougie Poynter. Ellie and Dougie dated on and off for years until 2016. The Love Me Like You Do singer's earliest known boyfriend is DJ Greg James, who she dated for nearly two years.  

In Ellie's early years, the singer romanced DJ Skrillex, actor Jeremy Irvine, and Calvin Harris. Despite fans' extensive theory on her romantic stint with Ed Sheeran, Goulding has vehemently denied the dating rumor between her and Ed.

Fans believed the Brit dated Ed at one point and cheated on him with Niall Horan. While Goulding admits to going on a few dates with the One Direction fame, she has refuted the numerous claims about her involvement with Sheeran. 

The Grammy nominee has even gone to lengths to say that the vicious rumor caused her a lot of trauma and hurt her career.
