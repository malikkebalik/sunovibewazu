    Michael Yo Facts

  • He appeared as a judge on Netflix’s baking competition show Is It Cake? in 2022.
  • In 2020, Michael participated in the 15th season of America’s Got Talent by performing stand-up comedy in front of judges Howie Mandel, Sofia Vergara, Heidi Klum, and Simon Cowell. He advanced until the quarter-final round before getting eliminated from the competition.
  • He played football while studying at the University of Arkansas. However, his football dreams ended because of multiple concussions.
  • Michael was one of the contestants on season 1 episode 7 of Fear Factor which aired on July 23, 2001. He had to perform stunts like free-falling from a 100-foot-high hotel window, being suspended upside down in a water tank, eating sheep’s eyes, etc.
  • Among the many celebrities he has interviewed are Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Jennifer Aniston, Kevin Hart, Dwayne Johnson, Jo Koy, Tone Bell, Dane Cook, Patton Oswalt, Jim Jefferies, Margaret Cho, Serinda Swan, Erik Griffin, Nicole Scherzinger, Warren G, Fran Drescher, Ashlee Simpson, and many others.
  • Michael has had small parts in films such as From Justin to Kelly (2003), Losing Control (2011), Bling Ring (2013) and television series such as Amber Brown (2022), Happy Together (2019), Starter Pack (2018), Kevin Can Wait (2017-2018), etc.
  • In 2016, he received a Daytime Emmy Award nomination for ‘Outstanding Entertainment News Program’ for the newsmagazine program, The Insider.
