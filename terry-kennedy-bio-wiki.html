Terry Kennedy Bio (Wiki)

Who is Terry Kennedy?

Terry Kennedy is an American professional skateboarder and artist.

He rides his skateboard with a regular footed stance. Terry is the Co-founder of ‘Fly Society’ which is a clothing and a music brand.

His main sponsors include Baker Skateboards, Active Ride shop, Shake Junt, Venture, Gold Wheels and FootPrint Insoles.

Terry Kennedy: Birth facts, Family and Childhood

He was born on May 27, 1985, in Long Beach, CA. He has a birth sign, Gemini.

Terry holds American citizenship but his ethnicity is unknown. He has not revealed any detail about his childhood and family.

Terry Kennedy: Education History

There is no education history available in his name but it is under review.

Terry Kennedy: Professional Life and Career

He started skateboarding at Cheery Park in downtown Long Beach.

He made his appearance on Bam Margera’s MTV television shows, ‘Viva La Bam, Bam’s Unholy Union’ and in Rob Dyrdek’s MTV show ‘Rob Dyrdek’s Fantasy Factory’.

Terry made his cameo appearance in Snoop Dog’s chart-topping song ‘Drop It Like It’s Hot’ and in the video for Mistah Fab’s song ‘Ghost Ride It’.

He also appeared non-ABC Family’s show ‘Switched!’ and appeared in the skateboarding video game ‘Skate’ and its sequels ‘Skate 2’, ‘Skate 3’ and ‘Skate It”. He also played on the documentary Beef IV where he discussed a beef he had with Lupe Fiasco.

He played in various films such as ‘Ice Cream VOL. 1’, ‘Baker 2G’, ‘Summer Tour 2001’, ‘Beaker has a Deathwish’ ‘Sweet Dreams’, etc.

He was shot twice while leaving a party in Long Beach in June 2005. He was shot once in the forearm and once in the jaw. terry is now fully recovered.
