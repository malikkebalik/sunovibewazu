Tilman J. Fertitta Wiki Biography

Tilman Joseph Fertitta, born on June 25, 1957, in Galveston, Texas USA, is a well-known entrepreneur, businessman, and the owner and CEO of Landry’s, Inc., a corporation that oversees multiple brands in dining, hospitality, entertainment, and gaming. He has been inducted into the Texas Business Hall of Fame and serves as the chairman of the Board of Regents of the Houston University System. Fertitta gained further recognition as a television personality after appearing in the reality TV show “Billion Dollar Buyer” in 2016. His business ventures have been active since the early 1980s.

According to authoritative sources, Fertitta’s net worth is estimated to be $2.6 billion as of mid-2016. In the Forbes 400 list of wealthiest Americans, he holds the 235th position, and the same magazine refers to him as the World’s Richest Restaurateur.

He grew up in Galveston, Texas, where his father owned a seafood restaurant. Fertitta started working at a young age, peeling shrimps at the restaurant. He later pursued his education at the University of Houston and Texas Tech University, majoring in hospitality management and business administration.

His career as an entrepreneur began with promoting and selling the natural nutrition supplement Shaklee. Subsequently, he ventured into running a construction business and establishing the Key Largo Hotel. In 1980, he co-owned the first Landry’s Restaurant, Landry’s Seafood. Over time, the company expanded to control more than 500 gaming, hospitality, and restaurant establishments, including Vic & Anthony’s Steakhouse, Mastro’s Restaurants, The Oceanaire, and many others. Landry’s Inc. currently employs over 50,000 people and remains the main source of Tilman Fertitta’s net worth.

Additionally, Fertitta participated in the reality TV show “Billion Dollar Buyer” in 2016, which aired on CNBC. The show, consisting of six episodes, featured Fertitta visiting small businesses to give them the opportunity to partner with Landry’s Inc., and received high audience ratings.

Fertitta is married to Paige Fertitta, and they have four children. The family resides in River Oaks, Houston. Apart from his business endeavors, Fertitta actively engages in public service, fundraising, and supporting various charity organizations. He serves as the chairman of the Houston Children’s Charity and holds memberships in several boards and organizations, including the Houston Police Department’s Police Foundation, the Houston Livestock Show and Rodeo, and the Museum of Fine Arts, Houston.
