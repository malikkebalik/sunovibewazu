Guaynaa Bio (Wiki)

Who is Guaynaa?

Jean Carlos Santiago Pérez famously as Guaynaa is a Puerto Rican rapper and singer. Guaynaa is best known for his unique style of music, which combines reggaeton, trap, and hip-hop.

His lyrics often feature explicit themes and sexual innuendos. But he also promotes positive messages of self-love and body positivity.

The Puerto Rican Rapper Guaynaa is well known for his song “Rebota” which became the first song to reach the Billboard Hot Latin Songs chart. He is now in contract with Universal Music Latino.

Guaynaa – Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Jean Carlos Santiago Pérez was born on September 16, 1992, in Caguas, Puerto Rico. He was born to a Cuban mother and a Puerto Rican father.

His father was a construction worker and his mother was a homemaker. Guaynaa’s ethnicity is Puerto Rican, and he is fluent in both Spanish and English.

In terms of education, Guaynaa attended the University of Puerto Rico, where he studied business administration. However, he eventually dropped out to pursue his career in music full-time.

Guaynaa – Professional Life, Careers

Guaynaa’s career in music began in 2017 when he released his debut single “Monte Gang,” which quickly gained popularity in Puerto Rico.

However, his career launched after his 2019 hit song “Rebota” to international fame. The song went viral on social media, racking up millions of views on YouTube and TikTok, and earning Guaynaa a spot on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs chart.

Since then, Guaynaa has released a string of successful singles, including “Chicharrón,” “Buyaka,” and “Mera.” He has collaborated with several high-profile artists, such as Jowell & Randy, Nicky Jam, and Sech.

In 2022, Guaynaa released his debut studio album, “BRB,” which features 14 tracks and includes collaborations with Karol G, Anitta, and Bad Bunny.

Guaynaa – Net worth, Salary

As of 2023, Jean Carlos Santiago Pérez’s estimated net worth is around $5 million.

He earns money from his music career, as well as from endorsements and sponsorships with companies such as Adidas and Pepsi.

Guaynaa – Rumors, Controversy

The famous rapper Guaynaa has been involved in several controversies throughout his career.

In 2020, he faced criticism for his song “Rompe Rodillas,” which some people interpreted as promoting violence against women. Guaynaa defended the song, saying that it was meant to be a satire of toxic masculinity.

In 2021, Jean Carlos Santiago Pérez was accused of plagiarism by Venezuelan rapper Neutro Shorty, who claimed that Guaynaa’s song “Chicharrón” copied elements of his own song “Borracho de Amor.” Guaynaa denied the accusations and said that the songs were completely different.

Body Features- Height, Weight

Guaynaa is 5 feet 8 inches (173 cm) tall and weighs around 67kg (147 lbs). He has a muscular build, dark brown hair, and brown eyes.

Social Media

Guaynaa is very active on social media, with over 6.5 million followers on Instagram and over 3.4 million subscribers on YouTube.

He regularly posts updates about his music, as well as photos and videos of his daily life. Guaynaa is famous for his playful and humorous personality on social media, often posting memes and jokes for his fans to enjoy.
