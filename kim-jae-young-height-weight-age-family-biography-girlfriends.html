Kim Jae-young Facts

  • The first movie that he watched was Memento, a 2000 American neo-noir mystery thriller film written and directed by Christopher Nolan.
  • Kim Jae-young was seen in the supporting role of Dae-chan in the 2013 sports film No Breathing.
  • He weighed more than 100 kg during his school years.
  • In 2017, he was cast as Jung Hyun-tae (Jeffery Jung), a successful travel writer and owner of a book cafe who has a crush on Yoo-mi, in the television romantic comedy series My Secret Romance.
  • Alongside Seol In-ah, Jo Yoon-hee, Yoon Park, and Oh Min-suk, Kim Jae-young co-starred as Goo Joon-hwi in the television romantic comedy-drama series Beautiful Love, Wonderful Life from 2019 to 2020. This role won him the 33rd KBS Drama Award in the “Best New Actor” category in 2019.
  • Kim Jae-young brought to life the character of Yoon Seon-woo in the television thriller drama series Secret Boutique in 2019 and this performance got him nominated at the 27th SBS Drama Awards in the “Best New Actor” category.
  • He appeared as Jun Woo-sung in the 2019 action crime film Money, starring Ryu Jun-yeol, Yoo Ji-tae, and Jo Woo-jin.
  • He has a long-haired Chihuahua.
  • Kim Jae-young prefers summers to winters.
  • He enjoys exercising, hiking with his parents, and watching movies.
  • He has been on a watermelon diet.
  • In 2021, Kim Jae-young played Seo Woo-jae, a sculptor and Hae-won’s senior from art school, in the television drama series Reflection of You.
