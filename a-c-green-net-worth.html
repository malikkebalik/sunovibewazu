A. C. Green Net Worth

A.C. Green Net Worth

A.C. Green, an American retired professional basketball player, has a net worth of $15 million. He played college basketball at Oregon State University, where he was an All-Pac-10 First Teamer from 1983 to 1985. Green was named Pac-10 Conference Player of the Year in 1984 and was a third team All-American in 1985. He was selected #23 by the Los Angeles Lakers in the 1985 NBA Draft and won three NBA championships with the team in 1987, 1988, and 2000. Green was also selected as an NBA All-Star in 1990 and was a member of the NBA All-Defensive Second Team in 1989.

Green played for several other teams during his career, including the Phoenix Suns, Dallas Mavericks, and Miami Heat. He set the record for playing the most consecutive games in the history of the NBA and ABA in 1,192. Green is also a member and endorser of Financial Destination, Inc. and is a co-founder of myWinningFactor. He runs youth camps through the A.C. Green Youth Foundation and married Veronique in 2002.
